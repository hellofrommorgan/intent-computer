# the anxiety before speaking is the same anxiety before writing

The physical response is identical — tightened chest, shallow breathing, the urge to delay. Whether the audience is a room full of people or a blank page, the body treats them the same.

This suggests the mechanism isn't really about the audience at all. It's about [[exposure to judgment]] — real or imagined.
